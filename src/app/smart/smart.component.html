UTILITY BILLS:

The amount a household or office is expected to pay for electricity, water and/or gas each month. Utility bills vary according to one's usage. Utility bills deal with household and office related daily bill payments. Under utility bills, customers can pay electricity Bills, Water Bills, Subscription Payments, Broadband and Landline Payments.


MOBILE PAYMENTS:

Paying Mobile Bills On time can allow us to enjoy Unlimited Calls, Messages and Data Usage etc. Mobile payments refers to paying bills of mobile networks, it may be a pre paid or a post paid bill Payment of any mobile network like Idea, Airtel, Vodafone etc. At Republic centre generally mobile payments are point-of-sale made payment through mobile device.


DTH RECHARGE & SALES:

Direct-to-Home, It is a digital satellite service that provides television viewing services directly to subscribers through satellite transmission anywhere in the country. In this a dish is placed outside a home which helps in receiving the signals and broadcasting the transmission onto a telvision. Ex: Airtel, Videocon, Reliance, Dish TV, Sun direct, Tata Sky etc.


TEMPORARY PERMIT FEE:

Temporary permits refer to a written or oral authorization to do something for a short duration. They are permits issued temporarily. Usually they are issued on payment of a prescribed fee for a specific period beyond which it has no validity. It is issued for any kind of Road Vehicle, when the vehicle is going roam in some new State/place for a short duration of time. At Republic Centre people can pay their Temporary Permit Fee to avoid the waiting at Check Post of any Border.


TAX PAYMENTS:

Tax is an amount of money that you have to pay to the government so that it can pay for public services. When a person or company is taxed, they have to pay a part of their income or profits to the government. When goods are taxed, a percentage of their price has to be paid to the government. At Republic centre, People can pay their tax payments like municipal tax, water tax etc.


AIRTEL MONEY:

Airtel Money is an account on your phone, in which you can load money and then use it for a host of service.You can send money, pay bills and recharge, directly from your mobile. You can send money to family and friends, recharge your prepaid mobile and digital TV, pay mobile / utility bills, book movie tickets, pay for your shopping (both at shops & online) and much more with Airtel Money.


E-CHALLAN FOR POLICE DEPARTMENT:

Challan is an official form or document such as receipt or Invoice. Police e-Challan is issued for someone an official notice of any traffic offence.
